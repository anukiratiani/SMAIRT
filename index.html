Help & Examples

Arithmetic Operations:

  • `5 + 3`
  • `10 - 4`
  • `6 * 7`
  • `12 / 3` or `12:3`
  • `Result: $5 / 2 = \frac{5}{2}$` (for fractions)

Algebraic Equations:

  • **Linear:** `solve 2x + 5 = 10`
  • **Quadratic:** `solve x^2 - 4x + 4 = 0`
  • **Quadratic (with fractions/decimals):** `solve 0.5x^2 + 1/2x = 3`
  • **Evaluation:** `evaluate x^2 + 3x for x = 5`

Calculus:

  • **Derivatives:**
    • `derivative of x^3`
    • `d/dx sin(x)`
    • `derivative of e^x`
    • `d/dx ln(x)`
  • **Integrals (basic forms):**
    • `integrate x^2 dx`
    • `∫ 1/x dx`
    • `integrate sin(x) dx`
    • `∫ e^x dx`
    • `integrate sin(x)cos(x) dx`

Statistical Calculations:

For these, type the numbers separated by spaces or commas in the input field, then click the relevant button (or type the query directly).

  • **Using buttons (after entering numbers, e.g., "1 2 3 4 5"):**
    • Click "Average" button
    • Click "Median" button
    • Click "Mode" button
    • Click "Range" button
  • **Direct query:**
    • `average of 10, 20, 30`
    • `median of 1, 5, 2, 8, 3`
    • `mode of 1, 2, 2, 3, 4`
    • `range of 10, 50, 5`
    • `standard deviation of 1, 2, 3, 4, 5`
    • `variance of 10, 12, 14, 16`

Combinatorics:

  • `combinations of 5 choose 2`
  • `permutations of 7 choose 3`

Other:

  • `biggest two-digit number`
  • `largest number 10, 20, 5`

Feel free to experiment! If I don't understand, I'll let you know.
