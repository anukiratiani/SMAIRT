Welcome to SMAIRT

SMAIRT solves advanced mathematical and statistical queries interactively.

How to Use

  • Enter queries in the Problem Input tab (e.g., 'avrge of 4 and 6'), use symbols, then press Enter or click Submit.
  • Click the ▼ arrow to access statistical buttons (Average, Mode, Median, Range).
  • View past queries in the History tab.
  • Use Shift+Enter for new lines.

Examples

  • Descriptive: "biggest two digited number"
  • Statistics: "average of 4 and 6", "median of 1, 3, 5", "mode of 1, 2, 2, 3", "range of 1 and 5"
  • Algebra: "solve x^2 - 4 = 0"
  • Calculus: "∫x^2 dx"
