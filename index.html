Help & Examples

Here are some examples of commands SMAIRT can understand:

  • Arithmetic: `5 + 3`, `10 * 4`, `9 / 3`, `15 : 5`
  • Linear Equations: `solve 2x + 9 = 17`, `5x - 1 = x + 7`, `x + 1/2 = 3/4`
  • Quadratic Equations: `solve x^2 - 4x + 4 = 0`, `x^2 - 9 = 0`, `2x^2 + 5x = 3`
  • Function Evaluation: `evaluate x^2 + 3x for x = 5`
  • Derivatives: `d/dx x^2`, `derivative of sin(x)`
  • Integrals: `∫x^2 dx`, `integrate 1/x dx`, `∫sin(x)cos(x) dx`
  • Basic Statistics: `average of 10, 20, 30`, `median of 1, 5, 2, 8, 3`, `mode of 1, 2, 2, 3`
  • Advanced Statistics: `standard deviation of 10, 12, 15, 13`, `combinations of 5 choose 2`
  • Descriptive: `biggest two-digit number`
