Welcome to SMAIRT

SMAIRT solves mathematical problems interactively, from algebra to Olympiad tasks.

How to Use

  • Describe a problem in the Problem Input tab or use the symbol table, then press Enter or click Submit.
  • Click symbols (∫, ^2, ^, √, π, log, ln, lg, dx) to insert them.
  • View past queries in the History tab.
  • Use Shift+Enter for new lines.

Examples

  • Algebra: "Expand (a + b)^2" or "Factor a^3 – b^3"
  • Calculus: "Integrate x^2 from 0 to 1"
  • Geometry: "Perimeter of a rectangle with sides 5 and 3"
  • Bases: "Convert 1010 base 2 to base 10"
  • Olympiad: "Find all integers n such that n^2 + 3n + 5 is a perfect square"
  • Square root: "Find √16"
  • Logarithms: "Find ln(2)"
